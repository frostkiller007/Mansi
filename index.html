About Us

Welcome to Heavenly Crumbs, where sweet dreams come true! We're a beloved destination in NYC, known for our exceptional pastries and commitment to quality. With a team of skilled artisans, we craft delightful treats using the finest ingredients. From flaky croissants to velvety cakes and delicate macarons, our creations are a symphony of flavors. But it's not just about the pastries; it's about the warm and inviting atmosphere that makes Heavenly Crumbs, a gathering place for friends and families. We're always exploring new flavors and accommodating dietary preferences with gluten-free, vegan, and allergy-friendly options. So, come indulge in our extraordinary world of pastries and let us create sweet memories together at Heavenly Crumbs.
